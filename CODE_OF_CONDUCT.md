# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on what's best for the project
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Contributors who do not follow the code of conduct may be banned from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open an issue or contact the maintainer directly.
